✦ Troubleshooting

My image won't upload

  • Ensure the file is a supported format: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, or AVIF.
  • Images over 20 MB are not supported. Try compressing the image first.
  • Try dragging and dropping instead of using Browse Files.
  • If the issue persists, try a different browser.

Export looks different from the preview

  • Refresh the page and re-upload your image - a previous bug caused this and has been fixed.
  • Ensure your settings haven't changed since the last preview render.

Quality slider is greyed out

  • This is expected for PNG (lossless format - quality doesn't apply). Switch to JPEG or WebP to use the slider.

Controls are cut off on my screen

  • The right-hand panel is scrollable - scroll down inside it to see all controls.
  • Try zooming your browser out (Ctrl/Cmd + −) to fit everything on screen.

Download isn't starting

  • Check your browser's download permission settings.
  • Make sure you've uploaded an image first - Save Image does nothing without one.

The page won't load

  • Clear your browser cache and hard-refresh (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R).
  • Disable browser extensions that block scripts or third-party requests.
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled.

Have an idea or found a bug? Email support@squarepic.com with subject line "Feature Request" or "Bug Report" and include:

  • What you were doing when the issue occurred.

  • Your operating system (Windows, macOS, iOS, Android).
  • A screenshot or recording if possible.
