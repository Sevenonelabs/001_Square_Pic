✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert?

SquarePic supports converting files into the three most common and necessary web formats: JPG (great for photos), PNG (great for graphics and transparency), and WebP (the modern standard for web optimization).

Is there a limit to how many images I can convert?

No. Because SquarePic runs locally inside your browser cache, you can convert as many files as you need without hitting paywalls or upload limits.

Will converting a photo reduce its quality?

Not unless you want it to! You can set the export quality to 100% to ensure a lossless conversion, or dial it down if you explicitly want to reduce the file size.
