# Universal Page Structure

Every page MUST follow this exact structure.

No freestyle.
No "creative interpretation."

Consistency wins.

---

# Required SEO Structure

## 1. Title Tag

Format:

Primary Keyword | Benefit | Brand

Example:

Instagram Photo Resizer | Resize Images Online Free | Squarepic

Rules:

- under 60 characters preferred
- must include target keyword
- must include clear user benefit

Bad example:

Home

That belongs in prison.

---

## 2. Meta Description

Format:

Keyword + use case + trust + CTA

Example:

Resize Instagram photos online for free. Perfect for posts, stories, reels covers and profile pictures. No signup required.

Rules:

- 150–160 characters
- clear and searchable
- no keyword stuffing

---

## 3. URL Slug

Format:

clean and short

Example:

/instagram-photo-resizer

Bad:

/best-free-fast-super-tool-for-image-resizing-2026

We are not naming YouTube videos.

---

## 4. H1

Only ONE H1 per page.

Example:

# Instagram Photo Resizer

Clear and direct.

---

## 5. Hero Section

Must include:

- short intro paragraph
- strong CTA
- trust indicators

CTA examples:

- Upload Image
- Resize Now
- Start Free

Trust indicators:

- No Signup Required
- Free to Use
- Secure Uploads
- Instant Download

Humans panic easily.
Reassure them.

---

## 6. Tool Preview Section

Must show:

- screenshot
- upload interface
- visual product preview

Users trust screenshots more than promises.

As they should.

---

## 7. How It Works

Exactly 3 steps.

Example:

Step 1 - Upload your image  
Step 2 - Choose dimensions  
Step 3 - Download instantly

Not 14 steps.
This is not tax filing.

---

## 8. Common Dimensions Section

Must include exact dimensions table.

Example:

| Use Case | Size |
|---|---|
| Instagram Post | 1080 x 1080 px |
| Story | 1080 x 1920 px |

This captures strong search intent.

Very important.

---

## 9. Benefits Section

Examples:

- preserve image quality
- fast resizing
- no watermark
- no login needed
- supports JPG PNG WEBP
- mobile friendly

Avoid corporate poetry.

---

## 10. Use Cases Section

Examples:

Perfect for:

- creators
- students
- job seekers
- eCommerce sellers
- marketers
- businesses

Improves relevance and conversions.

---

## 11. Comparison Section

Example:

Why use Squarepic instead of Canva?

- faster workflow
- no login required
- lightweight editor
- focused utility tool
- instant export

Positioning matters.

Otherwise you're wallpaper.

---

## 12. FAQ Section

Minimum 5 FAQs.

Required topics:

- free or paid
- image quality
- image storage
- mobile support
- signup required

FAQ schema must be implemented.

Google likes this.
Sadly.

---

## 13. Internal Linking Section

Must link related pages.

Example:

Also try:

- YouTube Thumbnail Resizer
- Passport Photo Maker
- Profile Picture Maker

Internal links are mandatory.

SEO without internal linking is plumbing without pipes.

---

## 14. Final CTA

Strong close.

Example:

Resize Your Image Now

Upload your image and get perfect dimensions instantly.

[Start Free]

Never end weakly.

Never use:

Learn More

Nobody is learning more.

---

## 15. Schema Markup Required

Must include:

- FAQ Schema
- WebApplication Schema
- Organization Schema
- Breadcrumb Schema

Boring.
Essential.

Like taxes.

# Content Rules

## Minimum Word Count

Each page:

1,200–1,800 words minimum

No thin pages.

300-word pages are decorative failure.

---

## Uniqueness Rule

Every page must be meaningfully unique.

Do NOT duplicate content across pages.

Google notices.

Google is petty and correct.

---

## Writing Rules

- simple language
- short paragraphs
- benefit-focused
- no filler
- no long dashes
- use small dash (-) only if required
- avoid robotic repetition
- optimize for users first, SEO second

Readable wins.

---

# Internal Clustering Strategy

Example:

Main page:

/image-resizer

Supporting pages:

- /instagram-photo-resizer
- /youtube-thumbnail-resizer
- /profile-picture-maker
- /passport-photo-maker

Build topic clusters to create authority.

Google likes authority.

Humans pretend to have it.
