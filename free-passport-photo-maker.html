Governments are incredibly strict about photo dimensions. An incorrect aspect ratio is the leading cause of visa and passport application rejections. Use this guide to select the right size.

Document Type Required Dimensions Aspect Ratio
US Passport & Visa 2 x 2 inches (51 x 51 mm) 1:1 Square
UK Passport 35 x 45 mm 7:9 Portrait
Schengen Visa (Europe) 35 x 45 mm 7:9 Portrait
Indian Passport / OCI 2 x 2 inches (51 x 51 mm) 1:1 Square
