✦ When to Crop Your Photos

A good crop can save a bad photo. Here are the most common reasons to reframe your images.

Removing Distractions

Did a stranger walk into the edge of your perfect vacation photo? Use the crop tool to tighten the framing and remove any unwanted photobombers or messy backgrounds from the edges of your shot.

Improving Composition

Use the "Rule of Thirds" by cropping your image so the main subject sits slightly off-center. This instantly makes standard photos look far more professional and visually interesting.

Formatting for Print

Standard picture frames (like 8x10 or 5x7) require specific aspect ratios. Before sending your digital files to a pharmacy or print shop, use our tool to crop them precisely so nothing gets accidentally cut off by the printer.
