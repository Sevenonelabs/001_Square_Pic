✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size is a Twitter header?

The recommended dimensions for a Twitter (X) profile header are 1500 pixels wide by 500 pixels tall, which is a 3:1 aspect ratio.

Why does Twitter cut off my header picture?

Because Twitter uses a very wide 3:1 ratio, standard photos will get the top and bottom severely chopped off. Furthermore, your circular profile picture hides the bottom left corner. SquarePic allows you to pad the image horizontally so it doesn't get ruined by the crop tool.
