✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size should a Twitch profile picture be?

Twitch recommends uploading an image that is 256 x 256 pixels, though you can upload higher resolution squares (like 500x500) and Twitch will scale it down safely. The maximum file size is 10MB.

Why is my gaming logo getting cut off?

If your mascot or logo is rectangular, Twitch's circular crop will slice the edges off. Use SquarePic to add a solid color background (like black or your brand color) around your logo to turn it into a center-aligned square.

Is there a watermark?

No! We know streamers need clean branding, so our tool is 100% free and never applies watermarks to your downloads.
