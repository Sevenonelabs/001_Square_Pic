✦ Official LinkedIn Image Dimensions

LinkedIn has very specific and unusually wide requirements for their background images. Uploading a standard 16:9 photo often results in the top and bottom being aggressively cropped out. Use this cheat sheet to ensure perfection.

LinkedIn Asset Recommended Dimensions Aspect Ratio
Personal Background Banner 1584 x 396 px 4:1 (Wide)
Company Page Cover 1128 x 191 px ~5.9:1 (Ultra-Wide)
Profile Picture 400 x 400 px 1:1 Square
Shared Image Post 1200 x 627 px ~1.91:1
Article Header Image 1200 x 644 px ~1.9:1

Our tool allows you to pad your images to match these extremely wide requirements, meaning you never have to crop out your head or your company's logo when setting up your profile header.

✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the correct LinkedIn banner size?

The optimal LinkedIn personal background banner size is 1584 x 396 pixels. For LinkedIn Company Pages, the required cover image dimension is slightly different at 1128 x 191 pixels.

Why is my LinkedIn banner blurry?

LinkedIn automatically compresses files over 8MB or images that don't fit the exact aspect ratio. By using our tool to pad your image to the correct dimensions before uploading, you prevent aggressive compression.

How can I make a vertical photo fit as a banner?

Upload your vertical photo to SquarePic, select a wide aspect ratio, and use our blur background option. The tool will place your vertical photo in the center and fill the wide banner edges with a beautiful, cohesive blur.

Do I need to sign up to resize my banner?

No. Our LinkedIn banner resizer is entirely free and does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information.

Does this tool work for company page covers?

Yes. You can use the custom dimension selector to hit the exact 1128x191 aspect ratio required for corporate pages without cropping your company logo.
