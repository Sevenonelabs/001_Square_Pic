About SquarePic

Free & Open to Use No Account Required 100% Browser-Based
SquarePic is a lightweight, privacy-first image tool that lets you create perfectly square photos in seconds - right in your browser, with zero uploads and zero tracking.

✦ What Is SquarePic?

SquarePic is a free, browser-based photo editor designed for one specific job: making any image square, beautifully. Whether you have a wide landscape shot, a tall portrait, or a panoramic photo, SquarePic transforms it into a perfect square without awkward cropping.

It's built for content creators, photographers, social media managers, and anyone who regularly publishes images to platforms like Instagram, which traditionally favour square or near-square formats.

✦ The Problem We Solve

Most photos taken on phones and cameras are rectangular - but social media grids are square. The standard approach of cropping loses important parts of your image. SquarePic gives you three smarter options:

  • Blur Frame - Extends the image with a beautifully blurred version of itself as the border. The full image is preserved and the result looks intentional and polished.
  • Solid Color - Surrounds your image with a clean, single-colour border. Choose from a curated palette or pick any custom colour. Great for branded content.
  • Crop to Fill - Intelligently crops the image to fill the square canvas from its centre. Ideal when the subject is centred and you're happy to trim the edges.

✦ Key Features

  • Three framing modes - Blur Frame, Solid Color, and Crop to Fill.
  • Adjustable blur intensity — Control exactly how soft or dramatic the background blur is.
  • Border padding control — Adjust the thickness of the border from 0% to 40%.
  • Zoom level — Scale the inner image up or down within the canvas.
  • Rounded corners - Add subtle or dramatic corner rounding to the final image - all the way to a perfect circle.
  • Custom colour picker — Choose any colour for solid borders, with a curated swatch palette for quick selection.
  • Export formats — Download your image as PNG (lossless), JPEG, or WebP with adjustable quality.
  • Real-time preview — Every change updates the canvas instantly as you adjust the controls.
  • Drag & drop upload — Drop an image directly onto the editor to get started immediately.

✦ Built for Privacy

Your images never leave your device. Everything happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API — no server, no cloud, no uploads.

We deliberately chose a fully client-side architecture because we believe your photos are personal. You shouldn't have to trust a server with wedding photos, client work, or anything else just to make it square.

SquarePic collects zero analytics, uses zero cookies, and requires zero accounts. You open it, edit your image, download it, and leave. Simple.

✦ How It Works

SquarePic uses the browser's built-in HTML5 Canvas API to process images in real time. Here's what happens when you use it:

  • You upload or drag an image onto the editor.
  • The image is read locally using the FileReader API - it never touches a network.
  • The canvas is set to a square size matching the longest dimension of your image.
  • The selected background style (blur, solid, or crop) is drawn onto the canvas.
  • Your image is composited on top, centred, with your chosen padding, zoom, and corner settings applied.
  • When you click Save Image, the canvas is encoded as your chosen format and downloaded directly to your device.

The whole process happens in milliseconds, entirely within your browser tab.
