✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size is an Instagram Reels Cover?

An Instagram Reels cover displays at a full vertical 9:16 aspect ratio (1080 x 1920 pixels). It is vital to format your cover at this size so it looks appealing in the dedicated Reels tab.

Why does my Reels cover look zoomed in on my feed?

While the Reels tab uses a 9:16 vertical view, your main Instagram profile grid forces a 1:1 square crop from the direct center of your cover. Make sure your face or text is positioned in the direct center when using our tool.

How do I add a horizontal photo as a cover?

Upload your horizontal or square photo to our tool, select the 9:16 ratio, and let us add a beautiful blurred padding above and below your image. This ensures your landscape photo fits the vertical requirement perfectly.
