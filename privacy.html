Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2026 Last updated: May 2026 Zero Data Collected
SquarePic is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect no personal data, store no images, and track no user activity. This policy explains exactly what happens - and what doesn't - when you use the application.

1 Who We Are

SquarePic is a free, browser-based image editing tool that helps you create square-format photos for Instagram and other social media platforms. The application is independently developed and operated.

For any privacy-related enquiries, you may contact us at support@squarepic.com.

2 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, receive, store, or process any of the following:
  • Your images or files - Images you upload are never transmitted to any server. They exist only within your browser's memory for the duration of your session.
  • Your name, email, or contact details - No account or registration is required to use SquarePic.
  • Your IP address - We do not log or record your IP address.
  • Device or browser fingerprints - We do not fingerprint your device or browser.
  • Usage data or analytics - We do not track which features you use, how long you spend on the page, or any other behavioural data.
  • Location data - We do not request or infer your geographic location.
  • Cookies or local storage data - SquarePic does not set any cookies or use localStorage/sessionStorage to store information about you.

3 How Your Images Are Processed

All image editing in SquarePic happens entirely inside your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. When you upload an image:

  • The file is read locally using your browser's FileReader API and converted to a data URL - this never leaves your device.
  • The image is drawn onto an in-memory HTML canvas element for editing.
  • All transformations (blur, colour fill, crop, zoom, rounded corners) are applied directly on that canvas in real time.
  • When you export, the canvas is encoded as a PNG, JPEG, or WebP file and downloaded directly to your device.

At no point in this process is any image data sent to a server, cloud service, or third party. Once you close or refresh the browser tab, all image data is immediately and permanently cleared from memory.

4 Third-Party Services

SquarePic makes one external network request: loading the Inter typeface from Google Fonts. When your browser requests this font file, Google may log the request (including your IP address and browser type) in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy if you have concerns about this. If you prefer to avoid this connection entirely, you may use a browser extension that blocks Google Fonts requests - the application will fall back to the system default sans-serif font and remain fully functional.

No other third-party scripts, SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or tracking pixels are loaded by this application.

5 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

SquarePic does not use:

  • HTTP cookies of any kind (session, persistent, or third-party).
  • Web beacons, pixel tags, or clear GIFs.
  • Browser localStorage or sessionStorage for user tracking.
  • Analytics platforms such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar.
  • Advertising or remarketing tags.

Your browser's built-in cookie settings and "Do Not Track" preferences are therefore irrelevant to this application, as we have no tracking infrastructure to configure.

6 Children's Privacy

SquarePic does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, the application presents minimal privacy risk to users of any age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child is using this application to process inappropriate images, please refer to our Terms & Conditions regarding prohibited uses.

7 Your Privacy Rights

Under privacy regulations such as the GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), and similar frameworks, individuals have the right to access, correct, and delete personal data held about them.

Since SquarePic collects and stores no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, export, or delete. Your privacy rights are inherently protected by the application's zero-collection design.

If you have any concerns or wish to confirm that no data is held about you, please contact us at support@squarepic.com and we will be happy to confirm.

8 Data Security

Because SquarePic transmits no data to any server, there is no server-side data store that could be breached, hacked, or exposed. Your images and usage patterns never leave your device.

The security of your images depends entirely on the security of your own device and browser. We recommend keeping your browser up to date and using a reputable device security solution.

9 Links to Other Websites

This application may contain links to external websites (such as Google Fonts documentation or social media platforms). Once you leave SquarePic, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of any third-party websites.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the application, applicable law, or best practices. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of SquarePic after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
