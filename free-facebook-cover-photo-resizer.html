✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size is a Facebook cover photo?

A standard Facebook cover photo displays at 820 pixels wide by 312 pixels tall on desktop, and 640 x 360 pixels on smartphones.

How do I make my whole picture fit my Facebook cover?

Because Facebook banners are extremely wide, standard photos get cropped heavily. Use our tool to add background padding to the sides of your photo so the entire image fits within the wide banner shape.

Does this work for Facebook Group banners?

Yes. Facebook Group covers have slightly different dimensions (1640 x 856 pixels). You can use our custom sizing tool to pad your image exactly for group headers.
