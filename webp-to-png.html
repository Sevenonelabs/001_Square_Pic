✦ Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my image download as a WebP?

WebP is a highly efficient image format created by Google. Many modern websites convert their images to WebP automatically to save bandwidth. Unfortunately, many older desktop programs and editing tools still do not support opening WebP files.

Why should I convert WebP to PNG?

If you need to edit an image you downloaded from the internet in Photoshop or send it to a colleague who has an older computer, converting it to a universal PNG ensures maximum compatibility.

Do I need to download software to fix my WebP?

No! Just drag and drop your WebP file into SquarePic, select PNG from the tabs, and download your universally compatible image instantly in your browser.
