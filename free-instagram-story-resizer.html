✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size should an Instagram Story be?

The perfect Instagram Story size is 1080 pixels wide by 1920 pixels tall, which is an exact 9:16 aspect ratio. This ensures it takes up the entire mobile phone screen.

How do I fit a horizontal picture on my story?

If you upload a wide photo to Instagram, it will shrink into a tiny box in the center with harsh borders. Using our tool, you can pad the photo with a beautiful blurred background, turning it into a gorgeous 9:16 vertical asset.

Does this work for Instagram Reels?

Yes! Instagram Reels use the exact same 9:16 vertical formatting. You can use our resizer to format photos you intend to use as Reels covers or in slideshows.
