✦ Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I convert a PNG to a JPG?

PNG files are lossless and often massive in file size. Converting them to JPG allows you to dramatically reduce the file size, making them much faster to email, message, or upload to a website.

What happens to the transparent background?

JPG does not support transparency. If you convert a PNG with a transparent background to a JPG using SquarePic, the transparent areas will be automatically filled with a solid color (usually white).

Yes. Our converter works directly in your web browser. No files are uploaded to our servers, ensuring your data remains private and secure.
