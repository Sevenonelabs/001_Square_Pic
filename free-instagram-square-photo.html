✦ Frequently Asked Questions

Why make my photo square for Instagram?

While Instagram allows tall and wide posts now, your main profile grid still enforces a strict 1:1 square crop. By making your photo square *before* you upload, you guarantee that your profile grid looks perfectly aligned.

How do I fit a full picture on Instagram without cropping?

Upload your full landscape or portrait photo to SquarePic. Select the 1:1 ratio. The tool will automatically add white borders (or a blurred background) to fill the empty space, turning it into a square without cutting off the edges of your photo.

Can I use white borders instead of blur?

Yes! Our tool lets you choose between a sleek background blur or clean, solid colors like white or black to frame your image.
