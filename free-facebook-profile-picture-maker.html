✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size is a Facebook profile picture?

Your profile picture displays at 170 x 170 pixels on a desktop and 128 x 128 pixels on smartphones. It is highly recommended to upload a larger square photo (like 500 x 500 pixels) to ensure the image remains sharp.

Why does Facebook crop my picture into a circle?

Facebook, like most modern social networks, uses a circular mask for profile photos in the news feed. To prevent your face from being cut off, use our tool to pad your photo into a square, keeping your face centered in the safe zone.

Do I have to crop my family out of the photo?

No! If you want to use a wide group photo as your profile picture, our tool will add padding to the top and bottom, effectively turning the wide rectangle into a perfect square without removing anyone from the frame.

Is my photo safe when I use this tool?

Yes. Formatting your Facebook profile picture using SquarePic happens entirely inside your browser. We never upload your personal photos to a remote server.
