✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What size should my LinkedIn profile picture be?

LinkedIn recommends uploading a square image (1:1 ratio) that is exactly 400 x 400 pixels. Larger file sizes up to 8MB are accepted as long as they are square.

How do I add a clean white background to my headshot?

If you have a vertical photo with an unappealing background, you can use our tool to pad the sides. Instead of a blurred effect, select a solid white or corporate hex color to create a professional studio look.

Why is my LinkedIn headshot pixelated?

LinkedIn compresses files heavily. If you upload a tiny picture and force the app to zoom in on your face, it will blur. Always format your high-resolution original file into a square using SquarePic before uploading.
