✦ Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I convert a JPG to a PNG?

JPG is a lossy format, meaning it degrades slightly every time it is saved. PNG is lossless, making it the superior format for graphic design, logos, and images containing text.

Can this tool make my background transparent?

While JPG files do not support transparency, converting an image to PNG format allows you to remove the background later in editing software, as PNGs natively support alpha-channel transparency.

Will the file size increase?

Yes, typically PNG files are larger than JPG files because they preserve far more data and do not use destructive compression.
