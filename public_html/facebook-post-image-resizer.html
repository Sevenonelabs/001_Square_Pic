✦ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best image size for a Facebook post?

For standard timeline posts, the optimal dimensions are 1200 x 630 pixels. However, square photos (1080 x 1080) also perform exceptionally well and take up more vertical real estate on mobile devices.

Why did Facebook cut off my picture?

Facebook automatically crops images that are extremely tall (like panoramic verticals) so they don't take up the entire screen. To prevent this, pad your photo into a standard square or 4:5 ratio before posting.

Can I format multiple photos for a carousel album?

Yes. If you are uploading multiple photos to a single post, it is best to format all of them as 1:1 squares using our tool first so they align perfectly in the album grid.
