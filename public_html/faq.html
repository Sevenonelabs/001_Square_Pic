✦ General Information

What is SquarePic and how does it work?

SquarePic is a premium, privacy-first web application designed to help you create perfectly square photos for Instagram, LinkedIn, and other social platforms without cropping out your original content. By adding intelligent blurred borders or solid backgrounds, SquarePic ensures your entire image fits into a square frame instantly, right in your browser.

Is SquarePic really free to use?

Absolutely. SquarePic is a 100% free tool for both personal and professional use. There are no hidden fees, no "premium" tiers, and we never add watermarks to your exported images. Our goal is to provide a high-quality, accessible utility for creators everywhere.

Do I need to register or create an account?

No. We believe in a friction-free experience. You don't need to sign up, provide an email address, or create a profile to use SquarePic. Simply visit the site, upload your photo, and start editing immediately.

Can I use the images I edit here for commercial projects?

Yes. SquarePic places no restrictions on the usage of your exported files. Whether you're creating social media posts for a brand, product photos for an e-commerce site, or personal memories, you are free to use the results. Please ensure you own the rights to the original image you upload. See our Terms & Conditions for details.

✦ Privacy & Data Security

Are my photos uploaded to your servers?

No. This is one of SquarePic’s core advantages. All image processing happens locally on your device using your browser's hardware acceleration. Your photos are never sent to a server, never stored in a database, and never seen by anyone but you. Your privacy is guaranteed by design.

Does this site track my activity or use cookies?

We value your privacy as much as you do. SquarePic does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or any form of user monitoring. The application runs entirely "client-side," meaning it operates independently of any data collection mechanisms. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is it safe to use SquarePic for confidential or sensitive images?

Yes, it is perfectly safe. Since your files never leave your computer or smartphone, SquarePic is an ideal tool for professionals handling sensitive client assets or individuals who prioritize their digital privacy.

✦ Using the Square Editor

What are the different squaring modes (Blur, Solid, Crop)?

Blur Frame: Our most popular option. It fills the square area with a beautiful, high-quality blurred version of your own image, creating a professional, seamless look. Solid Color: Allows you to choose a specific background color to frame your photo, perfect for maintaining a consistent brand aesthetic. Crop to Fill: If you prefer a traditional square crop, this mode intelligently centers your image to fill the entire square frame.

Which image formats are supported for upload?

SquarePic supports all modern image formats, including JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF (static), AVIF, and SVG. For optimal performance, we recommend files under 20MB.

What are the best export settings for social media?

We offer three export formats: WebP (Recommended, best balance of quality and size), JPEG (Best for standard photographs), and PNG (Best for lossless precision or images with text). For JPEG and WebP, you can adjust the quality slider from 10% to 100% to suit your needs.

Why is the quality slider disabled for PNG exports?

PNG is a lossless format, meaning it preserves every pixel perfectly. Because it doesn't use lossy compression, a "quality" setting isn't applicable. If you need to reduce file size, we recommend switching to WebP or JPEG formats.

I noticed a slight difference between the preview and the final export. Why?

While we strive for 100% parity, minor variations can occasionally occur due to how different browsers render CSS effects versus raw canvas data. If you notice a significant discrepancy, try performing the edit in a modern browser like Chrome or Edge for the most accurate results.

Is SquarePic compatible with mobile devices?

Yes! SquarePic is fully responsive and works beautifully on iOS and Android devices. You can "square" your photos on the go, directly from your phone's gallery, using the same powerful features available on the desktop version. Note: For mobile, use the "Browse Files" button as drag-and-drop support varies.
