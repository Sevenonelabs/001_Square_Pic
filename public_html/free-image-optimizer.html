✦ Frequently Asked Questions

Why is image optimization important?

Unoptimized, massive photo files are slow to upload to social feeds, eat up cellular data, and quickly fill your device's memory. Optimizing your images shrinks file size so sharing is instant.

What does an image optimizer actually do?

It removes unnecessary EXIF metadata, adjusts the color palette efficiently, and allows you to scale down the resolution and quality to save massive amounts of disk space.

No. SquarePic uses smart optimization to ensure the human eye cannot detect the compression. You maintain crisp visuals while shedding megabytes of unnecessary weight.
