✦ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fix the black bars on my YouTube thumbnail?

Black bars appear when your image is not in the exact 16:9 aspect ratio. You can fix this using our YouTube thumbnail resizer, which automatically replaces those ugly black bars with a professional blurred background or solid color.

What is the correct YouTube thumbnail size?

The optimal YouTube thumbnail size is 1280 x 720 pixels, with a minimum width of 640 pixels. It must maintain a 16:9 aspect ratio to look perfect across desktop and mobile devices.

Can I use a vertical photo for a YouTube thumbnail?

Yes. Our tool allows you to upload a vertical smartphone photo and pads the empty sides with a beautiful, seamless blur effect, turning it into a clickable, wide 16:9 thumbnail.

Is there a limit on how many thumbnails I can resize?

No limits at all. Our tool is completely free for unlimited use. You can process as many thumbnails as you need for your channel.

Will the tool compress my thumbnail and make it blurry?

No. The processing happens locally on your device, ensuring maximum resolution retention. Your subject will stay sharp, while only the background padding is blurred.
