Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026 100% Private
Your photos are your business. We built SquarePic as a privacy-first image editor. We do not upload, store, or view your images.

✦ Local Processing Only

Unlike other online editors, SquarePic does not upload your photos to a cloud server to edit them. Instead, we are a local image processing tool that uses your web browser to do all the work locally on your device. This means your images never leave your phone or computer.

✦ Zero Personal Data Collection

We do not require you to create an account or sign in to use our free square image maker. Because there is no signup process, we do not collect your name, email address, or payment information.

✦ No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use invasive tracking scripts to monitor how you edit your photos. We do not sell data to third parties because we do not collect any personal data to begin with.

✦ Security

The most secure way to process a photo online is to never transmit it over the internet. By processing everything locally, SquarePic ensures that your personal photos, business assets, and private images remain completely secure and under your control at all times.

✦ Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@squarepic.com.
